About Turere Salankat

Turere Salankat is a visionary leader committed to making Loita Ward a better place for all. With a background in community development and a passion for social justice, Turere is ready to serve and represent the people of Loita Ward.

OUR AGENDA AND MANIFESTO

  • Access to Quality Healthcare and Education
  • Empowering Youth and Women in Business
